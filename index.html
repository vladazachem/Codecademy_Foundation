About Us

We are a team at Foundation Coffee Roasters and we know everything about coffee

Greetings from the Foundation Coffee Roasters team! Our focus is specialty coffee that captivates from the first sip. Since 2015, we've been dedicated to the art of coffee roasting, partnering with some of the biggest names in the Ukrainian HoReCa market.

As we evolve, we're not only committed to advancing the Ukrainian coffee scene, but also setting our sights on European markets. Our mission is to spread the joy of exquisite specialty coffee to coffee shops, restaurants, as well as enriching the coffee experience at home.
